Now you can turn your wordpress blog into a money-making membership site. Charge for membership subscriptions, charge to read posts, offer trial periods etc.

Compatible with WP 2.8.2
-Does not use IPN.
-Purchase levels by category.
-Export user purchases to Excel.
-Using PHP redirect instead of javascript for post publishing.
-Allow edits of posts without losing credits.
-Subscriptions are managed by WordPress and not PayPal.

Help guide available under all posts in wp-admin
